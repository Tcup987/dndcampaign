Table Rules

  • Respect everyone at the table.
  • This campaign does not allow any of the following topics: sex/sexual assault, anti-LGBTQ+ sentiment, racism, sexism, or printers.
  • Come prepared with your character sheet and dice.
  • Communicate if you'll be late or absent with a minimum 24-hour notice. Sessions will run with one player absent; if more than one cancellation happens, the session will be postponed.
  • Have fun and embrace the story!

Character Generation Rules

These are the rules and guidelines for creating characters for this campaign:

  • Starting Level: New campaigns start at 3rd level. If joining later, you will be the same level as the current party.
  • Ability Scores: Rolled as a group, 4d6 drop the lowest. You can allocate each stat wherever you like; the rolls for this campaign are 5, 18, 15, 11, 16, 12.
  • Hit Points: Hit points are rolled on each level-up; 1s get rerolled once.
  • Healing Potions: Healing potions are a bonus action use item; however, you can use an action to gain the max healing roll instead.
  • Death Saving Throws: Death saving throws are rolled behind the DM screen between the player and the DM.
  • Allowed Sources: All base sourcebooks are allowed.
  • Custom backgrounds are allowed with DM approval.
  • No evil alignments.
  • Homebrew classes and subclasses require prior approval.
